Fountain of Homelessness

The top part of my art installation is a cage structure made with an umbrella and is covered with necessities to survive and documents “protecting” women, while this cage can provide shelter, it also locks whatever is inside. The bottom part of my art installation is a sculpture of a bruised woman’s belly, with hands reaching out. This symbolizes domestic violence women might face and their pregnancies. In the crack where the hand is coming from, there is a small pool of running water, designed like a small fountain. The water comes out from the top be pumped back up to the top. This water cycle symbolizes the cycle of homelessness women might face. Through this art installation, people can reflect on women’s conflicting situation between intimate relationships, resources, and societal norms
