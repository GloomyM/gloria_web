Fountain of Homelessness

Click Me

While the homeless population has not gained enough public attention, women in this group are especially the victims of homelessness. Unhoused women face a lot of difficulties that unhoused men might not encounter, such as lack of menstrual health care. Nobody wants to become homeless, but there are a lot of factors that can cause people, especially women, to end up homeless. I want my project to reflect different factors of homelessness and how many of these factors are specific to women.
